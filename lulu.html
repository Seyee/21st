Troll

A Short Story by Nicko Manipis de Guzman

Plot

The story "Troll" is about an unemployed millennial who applied for a job with an outsourcing firm, only to find out that they hire trolls.

Characters

  • Kiko: Snarky and foul-mouthed
  • Ronny: Conversational
  • Jessica: Always dramatic

Setting

TAYO Networking Inc. Main Headquarters, #1 Balimbing Street, WE Development Corporation Village Quezon City house.

Conflict

Person vs. Self: The protagonist's struggle between his ambition for wealth and his guilt for being a media troll.

Symbolism

The story is symbolized by a mask, representing how the character changes his personality to fit his job requirements.

Theme

Life is always full of ways to cope, and a change of lifestyle could be the start of greater things to come. Change begins with you, not with others. You should never pretend to be someone you are not.

Social Impact

This story provides social awareness about the impact and influence of social media.
