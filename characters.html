Meet Our Team

The Narrator/Protagonis

A college graduate struggling to find purpose and meaning, eventually employed as an online troll by TAYO Networking Inc.

The Narrator’s Mother

A supportive but initially demanding figure who pushes the narrator toward a more stable career.

Receptionist at TAYO Networking Inc

A cheerful guide who escorts the narrator to the interview and sets the tone of professionalism for the company.

TAYO Networking Inc.’s Employers

Anonymous voices communicating through laptops, representing the powerful and morally ambiguous nature of the company.

Kiko, Jessy, and Romy

Fictional personas created by the narrator to perform trolling activities online. Over time, they become so developed that they almost feel alive.

Manong Taxi Driver

A talkative driver who claims familiarity with TAYO Networking Inc. and its supposed benefits.

Other Social Media Users

Indirectly present, they interact with and are influenced by the personas created by the narrator.
