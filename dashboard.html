Story Analysis

"TROLL" Maikling Kuwento ni Nicko Manipis de Guzman

A highly stimulating short story that touches upon relationships'
intricacies in online contexts.

The world where online personas often oppose real-life identities is the
background of the story that touches on the issues of anonymity,
accountability, and the moral problems of internet culture.

This analysis will talk about the story's main parts such as themes,
characters, and narrative style, so the concealed messages and their
significance in present times can be revealed.
